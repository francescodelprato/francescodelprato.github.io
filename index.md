## Francesco Del Prato

I am a joint Ph.D. candidate in Business Economics between the [[https://imtlucca.it|IMT School for Advanced Studies]] in Italy and [[https://www.kuleuven.be/english/kuleuven/index.html|KU Leuven]] n Belgium. 

I am also a Research Fellow at [[CERGE-EI|https://www.cerge-ei.cz]] in the Czech Republic.

I am mainly interested in macro-labor economics with administrative microdata.
	My research currently focuses on _labor market dynamic_, _firms productivity_, and _public policy_ and primarily uses data from the [[https://www.inps.it/dati-ricerche-e-bilanci/attivita-di-ricerca/programma-visitinps-scholars|VisitINPS]] program.
